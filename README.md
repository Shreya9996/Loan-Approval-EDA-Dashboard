## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and **Interactive Visualization Dashboard** for **Loan Approval Prediction & Credit Risk Assessment**.

The dashboard helps financial institutions understand customer behavior, analyze loan applications, identify hidden patterns, and make better data-driven lending decisions.

It transforms raw loan data into meaningful insights using interactive charts and business intelligence visualizations.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

📌 Higher credit scores generally increase the probability of loan approval.

📌 Applicants with previous loan defaults have a higher rejection rate.

📌 Income plays a major role in loan approval decisions.

📌 Home ownership positively impacts applicant credibility.

📌 Education level influences borrowing patterns.

📌 Loan amount relative to annual income affects approval chances.

📌 Interest rate varies depending on loan purpose.
